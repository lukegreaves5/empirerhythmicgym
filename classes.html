CLASSES

WE WELCOME NEW MEMBERS


We offer a selection of classes for girls of all ages from beginner to elite. We are accepting new students every month. Call for a free trial.

Recreational Class
IS THIS CLASS FOR YOU?
The Recreational program is a wonderful introduction to rhythmic gymnastics. Recreation classes go through a detailed warm-up that will focus on increasing flexibility, strength, coordination & learning basic and safe body skills while utilizing an apparatus such as a rope, ball, hoop, & ribbon to music. Children 5 –12 years old can participate in this fun and skill building program. Gymnasts can participate in 1-3 recreational classes per week, offered 6 times per week.

Junior Olympic Program
IS THIS CLASS FOR YOU?
The Junior Olympic program is more rigorous and meets at least 3 days per week to prepare gymnasts to compete in Local, State, Regional, & National competitions. Team members are accepted on a tryout basis. As a team member, gymnasts are registered USA Gymnastics athletes. Upon coaches’ approval, gymnasts will be placed in Levels 3-9. Level placement depends on age, skill level and abilities, and dedication to training.

Xcel Program
IS THIS CLASS FOR YOU?
The Rhythmic Xcel program allows kids to have positive competitive experiences in an official USA Gymnastics setting with less training time required. The program also allows athletes to participate in rhythmic gymnastics while staying active in school and family activities.
